# AudioCast 🎙️

**Turn any PDF into an audiobook — right in your browser.**

No installs. No accounts. No uploads. Drop a PDF, pick a voice, hit play.

---

## Features

- **Drag & drop** one or more PDFs at once
- **Voice selector** — choose from all voices installed on your device
- **Speed control** — 0.5× to 2×, adjustable while playing
- **Live progress** — sentence-by-sentence tracking with a progress bar
- **Pause & resume** — picks up exactly where you left off
- **Space bar** shortcut to pause / resume
- **Download as TXT** — save the extracted text to your device
- Runs 100% in the browser — your files never leave your device

---

## Tips for best results

| Tip | Details |
|-----|---------|
| 🏆 Best browser | Chrome on Windows or macOS has the most natural voices |
| 📄 PDF type | Works on text-based PDFs only — scanned/image PDFs won't extract |
| 🖥️ Keep tab visible | Chrome may slow down speech in background tabs on long reads |
| ⌨️ Keyboard | Press `Space` anywhere on the page to pause or resume |

---

> Built with [PDF.js](https://mozilla.github.io/pdf.js/) and the Web Speech API.
